Controls
--------

WASD   = Move & Strafe
Shift  = Walk
Space  = Jump
Ctrl   = Crouch
LMB    = Shoot
RMB    = Aim
1      = Pistol
2      = M16
G      = Grenade
F9     = Restart
Escape = Exit
